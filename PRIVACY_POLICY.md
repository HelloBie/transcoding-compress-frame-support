# Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to **Transcoding+Compress+Frame** (the "App").

## 1. Summary

Transcoding+Compress+Frame is a local image and video processing app for macOS. The App is designed to process your selected files on your device.

We do **not** use third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or custom tracking systems in the App.

## 2. Information We Process

The App may process the following categories of information in order to provide its features:

- Files and folders you choose to open, preview, convert, or export
- Basic file metadata needed for processing and preview, such as file name, file type, size, dimensions, duration, and file timestamps
- Purchase and subscription status provided by Apple through StoreKit
- Limited local app state, such as purchase validation timing and premium access state stored on your device

## 3. How Your Data Is Used

The App uses data only to provide core functionality, including:

- Importing files and folders you select
- Previewing images and video frames
- Converting, compressing, resizing, or extracting frames from media you choose
- Exporting processed files to folders you choose
- Verifying premium purchases, subscriptions, restores, and offer code redemptions through Apple
- Saving limited local state needed to remember premium status and basic app behavior

## 4. Local Processing

Media processing in the App is intended to occur locally on your device.

Your selected files are used to generate previews and outputs directly on your Mac. Temporary working files may be created locally during processing and export. These temporary files are used only to complete requested tasks.

## 5. Purchases and Premium Access

If you purchase a premium feature or subscription, Apple may provide the App with transaction and entitlement information through StoreKit.

The App may store limited local purchase state on your device, including:

- Premium entitlement snapshot information in the macOS Keychain
- Premium validation date information in local app preferences

This local storage is used to improve premium access handling and validation between app launches.

Payment information is handled by Apple, not by the App.

## 6. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell your personal information.

We do **not** share your files with third-party advertisers or analytics providers.

The App may communicate with Apple services when needed for:

- In-app purchases
- Subscription status checks
- Purchase restoration
- Offer code redemption

## 7. Tracking

The App does **not** use app tracking for advertising purposes.

According to the App's privacy manifest:

- `NSPrivacyTracking` is `false`
- No tracking domains are declared
- No collected data types are declared for third-party tracking or analytics

## 8. Data Retention

Your selected media files remain under your control on your device unless you delete or export them yourself.

The App may retain limited local state, including premium entitlement cache data and validation dates, until:

- it is refreshed or overwritten,
- you clear app-related local data such as Keychain entries or app preferences, or
- the system removes such data.

## 9. Security

The App relies on Apple platform protections, including sandboxed file access where applicable, the macOS Keychain for limited purchase state, and Apple StoreKit services for in-app purchase validation.

No method of storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be 100% secure, but the App is designed to minimize data collection and keep processing local whenever possible.

## 10. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 11. International Users

If you use the App outside your home country or region, your use of Apple services such as StoreKit may involve processing by Apple according to Apple's own privacy terms and infrastructure.

## 12. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. When updated, the revised version will be posted at the Privacy Policy URL associated with the App.

## 13. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the contact method listed on the App's official GitHub repository page or app listing.
